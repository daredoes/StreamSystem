Restricted/Trusted

Some jobs require more trust than others. For example, we don't want just anyone working at the merch booth, since they handle a lot of our money.

You may mark a job "Restricted" to ensure that only "Trusted" staffers can sign up for it. You may mark a staffer as being "Trusted" on the Staffer admin page.

In general, avoid making staffers trusted unless there are specific Restricted shifts that you want them to be able to sign up for. Don't simply mark staffers as Trusted because you trust them.
