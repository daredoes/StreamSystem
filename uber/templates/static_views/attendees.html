Attendee Badges

Although we have lots of different badge options, a regular attendee badge is still an all-access, four-day pass to everything at {{ c.EVENT_NAME }}. This includes all of the things listed below, most of which are open 24-hours per day:
  • console room
  • LAN room
  • tabletop room
  • jam space
  • dealers room
  • video room
  • arcade cabinets, all on free-play
  • all of our nightly concerts
  • entry into any tournament
  • panels, presentations, contests, and game shows
  • special Challenges Booth
You can also kick in extra and get various bits of swag as thanks.
