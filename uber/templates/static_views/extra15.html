"Extra 15" Shifts

Some department chairs find it too chaotic to have one shift end just as another shift is beginning, especially when staffers show up late for shifts. This can leave a room desparately understaffed for a short time, as most of your staffers have left as their shifts end before the staffers who are supposed to start have arrived.

To combat this problem, you may create "extra 15" shifts, which make the shifts 15 minutes longer than their duration would indicate. So a 2 hour "extra 15" shift that starts at 3pm will actually have a staffer working from 3 - 5:15pm instead of from 3-5pm.
